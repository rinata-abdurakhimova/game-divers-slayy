# Shared Pitch

## Hook

`Make the score exactly 67 while Boss 67 tries to mathematically ruin you.`

## Theme Connection

The boss is the number `67`. The player wins not by attacking with a weapon, but by surviving arithmetic
pressure until the score becomes exactly `67`.

| --- | --- | --- |

## Demo Route

1. Start from a clean launch.
2. Show the placeholder cutscene.
3. Spawn in the safe sand area.
4. Walk and jump onto one block.
5. Trigger Boss 67.
6. Collect a helpful `+` pickup.
7. Dodge or demonstrate a dangerous multiplier.
8. Reach the first water event.
9. Show the water rule overlay and timer.
10. Reach exact `67` if possible, or show a prepared near-67 route.
11. Restart to prove stability.

## Fallback

If exact `67` is hard to reach live, use a rehearsed clip or screenshots showing:

- score changing from pickups/projectiles;
- `0` failure;
- water event;
- exact `67` victory.

Any teammate must be able to explain the rule in one sentence: `Avoid zero, use operations, hit 67.`
